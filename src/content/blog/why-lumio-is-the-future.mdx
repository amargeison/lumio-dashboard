## The Problem Nobody Talks About

Every growing business hits the same wall. You start with a spreadsheet. Then you add a CRM. Then a project management tool. Then an HR platform. Then an accounting package. Before you know it, you're running your business across twelve different tabs, three different logins, and a WhatsApp group that's somehow become mission-critical.

This isn't a technology problem — it's a fragmentation problem. And it's costing UK businesses thousands of hours and tens of thousands of pounds every year.

The average UK SMB uses between 8 and 12 different software tools to run their day-to-day operations. Each one was bought to solve a specific problem, and each one does its job reasonably well in isolation. But none of them talk to each other. Your sales data lives in one system, your HR data in another, your financial data in a third. Getting a complete picture of your business means manually stitching together reports from half a dozen platforms.

## The Connected Dashboard Vision

Lumio was built on a simple premise: **your business is one thing, so your software should be one thing too.**

Instead of bolting together a stack of disconnected tools, Lumio gives you a single, unified dashboard where every department — HR, Sales, Marketing, Operations, Support, Finance — lives in one place. Not as separate modules that happen to share a login, but as genuinely integrated systems where data flows naturally between departments.

When a new deal closes in your CRM, your operations team sees it immediately. When an employee submits a leave request, your project managers know about the capacity impact before it becomes a problem. When a support ticket reveals a product issue, your engineering team gets the signal without anyone needing to copy-paste a Slack message.

This isn't just convenient — it's transformative. Businesses that operate from a single source of truth make faster decisions, catch problems earlier, and spend dramatically less time on the administrative overhead that kills productivity.

## Automation That Actually Works

Most business software promises automation but delivers glorified if-then rules that break the moment anything unexpected happens. Lumio takes a fundamentally different approach.

Every workflow in Lumio is powered by n8n, an enterprise-grade automation engine that connects your internal processes with hundreds of external services. But unlike traditional workflow builders, Lumio's automations are pre-configured for the scenarios UK businesses actually encounter.

New starter onboarding? Lumio automatically generates the IT checklist, sends the welcome email sequence, notifies the line manager, and creates the probation review calendar entry — all from a single form submission. Leave request? The approval chain, capacity planning impact, and payroll adjustment happen without anyone touching a spreadsheet.
