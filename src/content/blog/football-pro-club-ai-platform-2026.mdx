## The Morning Briefing That Replaces Forty-Five Minutes of Admin

Every morning at 7:15am, the manager of a professional football club opens his phone and receives an AI-generated briefing that covers everything he needs to know before he walks through the training ground door. Squad availability. Injury updates with return timelines. Today's training session recommendations based on GPS load data. Upcoming fixture context. Transfer window deadlines. PSR compliance status. It arrives in a single view, written in plain language, with the ability to drill into any section.

Before this existed, that same information lived across five browser tabs, three phone calls, a WhatsApp group and a conversation with the physio in the car park. Lumio Pro Club was built to collapse that fragmentation into a single AI-powered operating system for the professional football club.

The briefing is not a static dashboard. It is generated dynamically each morning by an AI layer that ingests live data from across the platform — GPS loads from the previous session, medical room updates logged by the physio, financial thresholds approaching critical levels, and fixture schedule context. The result is a briefing that reads like a well-prepared chief of staff wrote it overnight. Except nobody did.

## Lumio GPS Integration: Automatic ACWR and Readiness Monitoring

At the heart of the physical performance layer sits Lumio GPS — session data flowing directly into the analytics engine, where it is processed against the club's historical baselines and surfaced in the context that matters. The integration is not a bolt-on. It is the platform.

The most critical metric is the Acute-to-Chronic Workload Ratio, known as ACWR. When a player's ACWR crosses the 1.3 threshold — the point at which research consistently shows elevated injury risk — the platform flags it immediately. The manager sees it in the morning briefing. The sports scientist sees it in the medical dashboard. The player's profile page updates in real time. There is no delay, no manual export, no CSV file sitting in someone's downloads folder.

This matters because the gap between data collection and data action is where injuries happen. A GPS device is only as useful as the speed at which its data reaches the person who can act on it. Lumio closes that gap to near zero.

## The FIFA-Style Pitch View with Click-to-Swap

Formation management in professional football has traditionally lived on whiteboards, in the manager's head, or in tactical software that exists entirely separately from the rest of the club's infrastructure. Lumio Pro Club introduces a FIFA-style pitch view that displays the current squad in formation, with the ability to click any player and swap them with any available alternative.

When a swap is made, the platform instantly recalculates the implications. Is the replacement player carrying a GPS load that suggests he should not start? Is his contract status about to trigger a bonus clause? Has he played fewer than ninety minutes in the last three weeks? The pitch view does not exist in isolation. It is connected to medical, financial and performance data, which means formation decisions are made with full context rather than intuition alone.

The set pieces library extends this tactical layer further, offering more than ninety pre-built routines that can be assigned, customised and shared with coaching staff. Each routine is visually mapped and can be filtered by situation — short corners, long throws, free kicks in the final third, defensive set pieces from wide areas. For clubs investing significant coaching time in set piece preparation, this removes hours of manual diagram work.

## PSR Compliance Tracking: Live, Not Retrospective

The Profitability and Sustainability Rules have changed the financial landscape of English professional football. Clubs operating under PSR face rolling three-year assessment periods with defined loss thresholds, and the consequences of breach range from points deductions to transfer embargoes. Despite these stakes, most clubs still track PSR compliance retrospectively, using quarterly financial reviews that arrive weeks after the period they cover.

Lumio Pro Club tracks PSR compliance in real time. Every transaction that affects the PSR calculation — amortisation of transfer fees, wage commitments, commercial revenue recognition — is logged and reflected immediately. The finance director sees a live PSR position. The board sees a simplified traffic light indicator. The manager sees a contextual note in his morning briefing when a proposed transfer would move the club closer to the threshold.

This is not financial software pretending to understand football. It is football software that understands finance.

## The Board Suite: Role-Gated Views for Every Stakeholder

A football club is not a single user. It is a chairman who wants a strategic overview, a finance director who needs granular detail, a director of football who lives in recruitment data, a head of medical who tracks twenty-three bodies through a forty-six-game season, and a commercial director managing fifteen sponsorship relationships simultaneously.

Lumio Pro Club's Board Suite provides role-gated views that surface exactly the information each stakeholder needs, in the format they expect, without exposing data that is not relevant to their function. The chairman sees headline KPIs, financial health indicators and a board meeting agenda builder. The director of football sees squad depth, contract expiry timelines and recruitment pipeline status. The head of medical sees return-to-play protocols, ACWR trends and upcoming fixture density analysis.

Every view draws from the same underlying data. There is one source of truth. But the presentation layer adapts to the role, which means board meetings become focused conversations rather than hour-long data reconciliation exercises.
