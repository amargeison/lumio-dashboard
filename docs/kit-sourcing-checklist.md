Goal: make **5–10 pilot kits** yourself, cheaply, to gauge coach uptake. DIY assembly
now; turnkey factory kitting only later at 100+. Don't custom-print the box at this volume.

## The box (the only hard part — don't custom-print at 5–10)

| Option | What | Cost | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A — recommended** | Stock black **rigid gift box** OR **pick-and-pluck foam case** + branded **printed sleeve / logo card / vinyl sticker** | £5–12/box all-in | Now, 5–10 kits |
| B | No-minimum custom-printed box (My Box Printer / Custom Packaging London) | high per-unit at 5–10 | Only if presentation is critical for the pitch |
| C | Custom magnetic rigid box (foil logo) from Alibaba | £3–8/box but **MOQ ~100** | Later, at scale |

Search terms (A): "black magnetic gift box foam insert", "pick and pluck foam case",
"rigid presentation box foam". Print-on-demand (no minimum): Vistaprint / Instantprint /
local printer — order a **belly-band sleeve**, a **logo card insert**, and a **vinyl logo
sticker**. The branded card + sticker does ~90% of the "premium" feel. Your mockup already
includes the logo card — lean on it.

## Process

1. **Order 1 sample of each** component (items 1–6) + 1 candidate box. ~£40–60, 2–4 wks.
2. **Check quality** — mic audio, keyring/dampener finish, box feel.
3. **Order pilot quantities** (buy ~12 for 10 kits — spares cover defects). Ask one seller
   to **combine shipping** where possible.
4. **Print** the sleeves / cards / stickers (no minimum, ~1 week).
5. **Assemble** — shape the pluck foam, drop items in, add card + sticker. One afternoon
   for 10.

## Cost tally (pilot, per kit)

Keyrings £4–7 · dampeners £1–3 · mic £8–15 · stand £2–5 · trophy £2–5 · shutter £1–3 ·
box+foam £5–12 · print £1–3 → **≈ £30–55/kit** at 5–10. (At scale ~100–500: **£20–30/kit**.)

## When to switch to turnkey (the trigger)

Once uptake is proven and you're ordering **~100+**: run an **Alibaba RFQ** for one supplier
to make the custom magnetic rigid box (foil logo) + source the items + **kit and ship
ready-assembled**. At that volume MOQs and per-unit costs finally work (custom box £3–8;
a sourcing agent's kitting fee becomes worth it). Until then, DIY wins.

## Tips

- **Always sample before bulk** — one bad mic batch ruins the kit.
- **Buy spares** (12 for 10) for defects/loss.
- **Combine sellers/shipping** to cut postage and lead time.
- Keep the mic and box **modest** in the pilot — they're the cost swingers.
- Amazon/UK for anything you need fast (pricier) vs AliExpress for cheapest (2–4 wks).
